In plain terms: the game keeps everything on your device and runs no servers of its own. The only data collected leaves through Google AdMob, which shows the optional reward ads.

1. Information the app itself collects

None that leaves your device. The app has no accounts and no sign-in. Your career progress, statistics, unlocked cosmetics, and settings are stored locally on your device only. We cannot see them, and they are not uploaded anywhere. Deleting the app removes this local data. We do not collect your name, email, contacts, location, photos, or files.

2. Local ("Versus") multiplayer

The optional Versus mode connects players over your local Wi-Fi network only (LAN). Game moves and the name and profile picture you choose for a session are sent directly between devices on that network to run the match. This data is not routed through any server we operate — we operate none — and is not stored after the match ends.

3. Advertising — Google AdMob

The app offers optional rewarded ads (for example, "watch an ad for bonus in-game cash"), served by Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google and its partners may collect and process:

  • your device's advertising ID and other device identifiers;
  • IP address and general (coarse) location derived from it;
  • device information (model, operating system, network state);
  • ad interaction data (ads shown, tapped, and rewards granted).

This processing is carried out by Google under its own policies, not by us. See the Google Privacy Policy, how Google uses information from apps that use its services, and Google AdMob & the advertising ID. You can reset or delete your advertising ID at any time in Android Settings → Privacy → Ads.

4. Analytics and other tracking

We do not use any separate analytics, crash-reporting, or tracking SDKs. The only third-party service in the app is Google AdMob, described above.
